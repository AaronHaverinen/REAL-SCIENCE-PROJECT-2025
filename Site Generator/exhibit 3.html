Exhibit 3: Mollusca

These are mollusca, a phylum that you're probably a bit familiar with. Their symmetry pattern is bilateral. Mollusca differ from other phyla because they actually have multiple hearts in their open circulatory system. Mollusca reproduce sexually through external fertilization, unlike most animals who reproduce internally. Some mollusks have shells and some do not, but all of them do have a mantle for protection.
